I have always had a deep appreciation and love for music. My dad has been instrumental in developing this sentiment, introducing me to all sorts of genres and eras of music throughout my life. In the fall of 2022, I began learning how to play the guitar through the internet, talented friends, and experimentation. It has been such a blast immersing myself in the world of music after previously focusing on athletics for most of my life. I started taking guitar lessons this fall through my university, and I hope to continue my learning and growth as a player.
